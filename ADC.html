Alzheimer’s & Dementia Care

Dementia is an umbrella term describing a collection of symptoms that affect memory, thinking, reasoning, and the ability to perform everyday activities independently.

TIPS, GUIDES & ADVICE
  • Maintain a consistent daily routine
  • Use simple and clear communication
  • Create a safe and familiar environment
  • Encourage mental activities like puzzles
  • Be patient and supportive always

Alzheimer’s & Dementia Care Communication Tips

  • Keep sentences short and simple
  • Speak calmly
  • Ask one question at a time
  • Use visual cues
  • Before you speak, get their attention first
  • Speak slowly

Common Alzheimer’s & Dementia Symptoms include:

  • Memory loss
  • Confusion with time or place
  • Mood or personality changes
  • Difficulty speaking or understanding
  • Wandering
  • Trouble with daily tasks
